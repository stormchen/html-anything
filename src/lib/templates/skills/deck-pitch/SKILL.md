【意圖】10 頁投資人 ready 的 fundraising deck。

- Problem · Solution · Why Now
- Product (截圖占位)
- Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Traction (柱状圖大數字)
- Business model
- Go-to-market
- Team
- Ask: $4.5M-style page
- Thanks / Contact
